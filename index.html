My Story

Hi, I’m Camden — the heart behind Blessed Amusements. Growing up with epilepsy taught me two powerful things: life is precious, and joy is worth fighting for. Every challenge nudged me toward a simple mission: help people feel happy. Not just once, but over and over.

That’s why I started Blessed Amusements. Our claw machines and arcade setups are more than games — they’re tiny joy engines. We deliver, stock, clean, and maintain everything at no cost to your venue. Your guests get a moment of wonder; you get a steady share of revenue with zero hassle. It’s a win-win that lets me live my purpose — bringing smiles to strangers, one play at a time.
